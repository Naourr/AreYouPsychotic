What even is Bipolar Disorder?


Bipolar disorder is like being on a rollercoaster that you never bought tickets for. One minute you're the CEO of happiness, the next you're crying over a bread commercial. Let's see how cooked you are.

Do you go from "I'm gonna change the world" to "I'm gonna eat drywall" in under 5 minutes?

Have you ever laughed so hard you cried, then cried so hard you laughed, then forgot why you started?

Do you make life decisions based on your mood, like dying your hair at 3am because "vibes"?

Do you ever feel like your brain is running Windows 98 and then suddenly switches to Windows XP?

Do you ever go from "I am a god" to "I am a potato" before breakfast?

Do you ever start 10 projects at once and finish none because you got distracted by a squirrel meme?

Do you ever feel like your life is just a series of "mood swing" TikToks?

Do you ever feel like your emotions are controlled by a cat walking on a keyboard?

Do you ever go from "I love everyone" to "I will fight everyone" in the same group chat?

Do you ever feel like your mood swings have mood swings?
