What even is Schizophrenia?

Do you ever hear your name being called, but it's just your brain trolling you?

Do you ever see something move in the corner of your eye and immediately assume it's a government agent?

Do you ever think the TV is talking directly to you and only you?

Do you ever think your phone is listening to you and sending memes to the government?

Do you ever see patterns in random things and think it's a secret message from the universe?
